| **Method**                  | **Problems It Finds**                                                                  | **Outcome / What You Get**                                                                             |
| --------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **User Interviews**         | Frustrations, unmet needs, unclear expectations, pain points in workflow               | Deep understanding of user motivations and challenges; insights to define problem statements           |
| **Observation / Shadowing** | Hidden behaviors, workarounds, inefficient workflows, context-specific issues          | Real-world evidence of how users interact with the product; uncovers problems users may not articulate |
| **Stakeholder Interviews**  | Misalignment between business goals and user needs, assumptions, technical constraints | Clear understanding of business priorities and constraints; helps prioritize UX issues                 |
| **Usability Testing**       | Confusing navigation, unclear labels, errors, slow task completion                     | Direct evidence of usability issues; actionable recommendations to improve design                      |
| **Data Analytics**          | High drop-off points, low engagement areas, feature usage patterns                     | Quantitative evidence of problem areas; metrics to measure impact of improvements                      |
| **Competitor Analysis**     | Missing features, poor experiences in similar products, user expectation gaps          | Benchmarking and opportunity identification; ideas for differentiation and improvement                 |
